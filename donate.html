Thank you for visiting our Donation page! Cal Parli (as we are known in the debate community) is one of the only student-run teams in the country! Since we are not designated as an organization, we don't receive as much funding as a university-integrated program. It is from generous donations from our Alumni and the tireless efforts of the members of the team that we continue to exist and compete at a national level. Aside from donations, Cal Parli raises money by judging local tournaments, working at the Cal Invitational, and running our own high school and college tournament.

As a 501c3, your donation to Cal Parli is tax-deductible! And with our page on Cal Giving, it takes only moments to give back! Your donation allows Cal Parli to do all of the following:

  1. Travel to local and national tournaments
  2. Pay for high registration fees
  3. Travel to Nationals (both NPTE and NPDA)
  4. Transportation and hotel costs
