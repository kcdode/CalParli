Support Us

Parli @ Berkeley is a completely student run organization. Any support to help us run the team is greatly appreciated!

Thank you for visiting our Support page! Cal Parli is one of the only student-run teams in the country! Since we are not designated as an organization, we don't receive as much funding as a university-integrated program. It is from generous donations from our Alumni and the tireless efforts of the members of the team that we continue to exist and compete at a national level. Aside from donations, Cal Parli raises money by judging local tournaments, working at the Cal Invitational, and running our own high school and college tournament.

As a 501c3, your donation to Cal Parli is tax-deductible! And with our page on Cal Giving, it takes only moments to give back! Your donation allows Cal Parli to do all of the following:

- Travel to local and national tournaments
- Pay for high registration fees
- Travel to Nationals (both NPTE and NPDA)
- Transportation and Hotel costs
