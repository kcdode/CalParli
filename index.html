PDB wins the Keeling Invitational Fall at Chabot College!

On October 4th, Parliamentary Debate at Berkeley traveled three teams of our new members to Chabot College’s Keeling Invitational Fall in Hayward, CA, just a stone’s throw away from Berkeley! With the coaching and leadership help of 2019 NPDA co-champion and team alumna Jessica Jung, PDB NS (Nikoubin & Shadmany) finished prelims at 3-1, and went on to win finals on a 2-1 and take home the tournament championship! Meanwhile, AH (Alpert & Huang) went 2-2 and nearly broke to elims. PDB BK (Bhasin & Kazim) also finished 1-3 in prelims at their first ever NPDA tournament!

PDB closes out the Rice University Classic

This past weekend, two Parliamentary Debate at Berkeley teams attended the Rice University Classic in Houston, Texas. Both teams advanced to finals after winning their semifinals rounds, closing out the tournament. Their success won PDB the 2nd Place School Sweepstakes, despite only sending 2 teams out of 24 total in the tournament. Shannon Bonet and Ryan Rashid (PDB BR) had a perfect tournament, going 6-0 in prelims and completing their elimination rounds without dropping a single ballot, making it to the finals after picking up against Rice University CS. Amanda Miskell and Brian Yang (PDB MY) went 5-1 in prelims and defeated University of the Pacific FL in semis to complete the tournament closeout. Brian, Shannon, and Amanda earned 3rd, 5th, and 6th Best Speaker respectively, while Ryan’s 11th Best Speaker ranking narrowly missed a top 10 spot. BR’s incredible run also places them as the current #1 team in NPTE rankings - a spot that they’ll be aiming to keep for the rest of the year!
