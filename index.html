PDB attends the Golden Gate Opener

Parliamentary Debate at Berkeley attended our first tournament of the season this past weekend, where we sent 12 debaters in 6 teams to the Golden Gate Opener at San Francisco State University. Out of the 12, 11 were first-time debaters for PDB! Sophomore Ryan Rashid was the only returning PDB member for GGO, partnering with freshman Shannon Bonet. Our teams did incredibly well overall: 5 of the 6 advanced to elimination brackets, and 4 debaters earned top 10 speaker awards. MM and NS made it to octofinals, JS and BR both reached quarters, and KZ reached semis before dropping to the tournament champions, Lewis & Clark MT. Detailed results are available on our records page. We thank SFSU for hosting GGO, and look forward to an exciting debate season!

PDB Wins Two National Championships!

Parliamentary Debate at Berkeley had an incredibly successful run at the NPDA championship tournmanets, NPTE and NPDA. PDB KT and PDB-WWU Hybrid CJ made it to finals of NPDA, closing out the tournament. At at NPTE, KT took a 7-0 final ballot against Rice TB in elimination finals to secure the championship. This marks the second year in a row of PDB's championship status in the NPDA circuit.
